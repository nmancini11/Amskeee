ABOUT AMSKIER INSURANCE

AMSkier is the largest direct insurer of children's camps and youth programs in the country, insuring camps and youth programs throughout the United States and around the world.

AMSkier has been insuring children's camps since the 1930s and is known for having "Broad Shoulders, Bright Ideas" that are exclusively available to AMSkier Clients.

AMSkier's Camp Program insures all lines of cover including property, liability, vehicles, workers' compensation, excess liability (SKI-brella,) camper medical, directors and officers, and more. AMSkier is a program administrator for A+ rated insurance companies with authority for binding, underwriting, claims oversight, and policy issuance.

ABOUT HAWLEY, PENNSYLVANIA

Nestled in the Pocono Mountains along Route 6, and situated between Honesdale and Lake Wallenpaupack, Downtown Hawley is the kind of quaint and friendly small town where one may arrive as a visitor and end up calling home. Hawley's strong sense of pride and its commitment to preserving its connection to the Delaware and Hudson Canal, the Lackawaxen River, and the Erie Railroad are evident throughout the community. Discover Hawley's celebrated heritage and its historical architecture. This family friendly, fun-filled hub is a place where kids of all ages can explore, learn, and experience all that Hawley has to offer.
