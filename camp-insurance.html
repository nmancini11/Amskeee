CAMP INSURANCE

AMSkier is the largest direct insurer of children's camps and youth programs in the country, insuring camps and youth programs throughout the United States and around the world.

AMSkier has been insuring children's camps since the 1930s and is known for having "Broad Shoulders, Bright Ideas" that are exclusively available to AMSkier Clients.

AMSkier's Camp Program insures all lines of cover including property, liability, vehicles, workers' compensation, excess liability (SKI-brella,) camper medical, directors and officers, and more. AMSkier is a program administrator for A+ rated insurance companies with authority for binding, underwriting, claims oversight, and policy issuance.

AMSkier also has its own re-insurance company which makes them unique. They are willing to put their capital at risk.

AMSkier has formed AMSkier Partners, Inc. where seasoned and experienced camp directors and some of the most qualified people in camping come together in their specific areas of expertise including inappropriate intimate behavior issues, legal issues, public relations situations, behavior issues, emergency preparation, security, and health care situations. AMSkier Partners, Inc. is always expanding with new professionals that are eager to become safety partners with every AMSkier Camp.

At AMSkier, the insurance transaction is just the beginning. These innovative programs become an important part of the AMSkier relationship with its camps. This includes The Gene Ezersky Camp Safety College, Camp-ALERT Network, SAFETYunderwriters, Broad and Bright newsletters, The Staffer Index, Safety Workshops, The AMSkier Foundation, Blue Skies, 866-SKIER WC, The Ben and Barbara Appelbaum Forums, AMSkier Partners, Inc. and a whole lot more.

We welcome opportunities to develop new relationships with camp directors (that may include your insurance providers).
