CAMP INSURANCE

AMSkier is the largest direct insurer of children's camps and youth programs in the country, insuring camps and youth programs throughout the United States and around the world.

AMSkier has been insuring children's camps since the 1930s and is known for having "Broad Shoulders, Bright Ideas" that are exclusively available to AMSkier Clients.

PERSONAL INSURANCE

Since 1920, AMSkier has been insuring homes, vehicles, boats, horses, recreational vehicles and just about anything else of value that you have and want to protect. Licensed in states across the country, AMSkier has earned a national reputation for insuring primary homes, secondary homes, and specialty vehicles. AMSkier prides itself for being able to find the best way to insure both the everyday and more challenging opportunities.

Learn More

BUSINESS INSURANCE

AMSkier insures a wide range of large and small businesses across the country. These businesses include banks, day schools, rental properties, manufacturers, bed and breakfasts, country inns, schools, student youth travel, hospitals, publishers, outdoor adventure programs, retail establishments, non-profit organizations, restaurants, destination spas, art galleries, community associations, resorts and other recreational businesses.
